This is either not implemented in ARIA or sfizz, or our testing was not able to discover how it works. The relevant opcodes are `egN_loop`, `egN_loop_shape` and `egN_loop_count`.

## Example

```
eg01_loop=2
eg01_loop_shape=1
eg01_loop_count=8
```
